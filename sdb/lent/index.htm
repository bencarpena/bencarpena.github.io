Lenten Message


  1. Dear Parishioners,

    The Lenten Season has started last Ash Wednesday. I was impressed by the number of churchgoers on that day wanting to start the Lenten Season seriously through penance symbolized by the imposition of ashes. Lent is a most wonderful time of the liturgical year because God gives us bountiful graces during this season.

    It is said that Lent is the springtime of the Church. Spring is a symbol of life. Thus, during this Lenten season, God gives us all the opportunities to renew our life.

    The goal of Lent is Easter. We are invited to look forward in celebrating Easter and share in the victory of Christ over sin and death. But we can share His resurrection only if we die with Him. The Lenten practices of penance, almsgiving and prayer are means in order for us to participate in Christ’s suffering.

    May I invite you to, therefore, to make these Lenten practices as your resolution to participate in the suffering and death of Jesus. According to St. Paul, “If we die with Him, we shall also rise with Him.”

    This is my prayer for each and everyone of you.


    Yours in Christ Jesus,

    Fr. Rolo Alcasid SDB
    Parish Priest
